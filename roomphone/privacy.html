Room Phone Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (the “Privacy Policy”) explains how the software mobile application entitled “Room Phone” (the “Mobile App”) that was created by Teecom, Inc., (“Company”, “we”, or “us”) collects, stores, uses, and discloses information from you when you use the Mobile App. If you do not agree with or are not willing to comply with any portion of this Privacy Policy, do not download or use the Mobile App.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

GENERALLY

The Mobile App is a software application for your phone, tablet or other mobile device (each, a “Device”) which enables users to easily control conference room phone systems and equipment (“Conferencing Equipment”). For example, the Mobile App enables users to add multiple conference rooms, dial contacts from the user’s address book, and manage in-call features such as hold, mute and call volume. Except for Device Information and Volunteered Information (each as defined below), the Mobile App does not export and we do not collect any information from you or your Device, including, without limitation, your Address Book Information.

ADDRESS BOOK INFORMATION

The Mobile App includes a “your contacts” feature (the “Address Book Feature”), which may use the phone numbers gathered from your Device’s address book (“Address Book”) to enable users to quickly auto-dial those numbers on Conferencing Equipment. The Address Book Feature is optional, however, if you choose not to allow the Mobile App to access your Device’s Address Book, then certain features of the Mobile App may not be available. When you install and launch the Mobile App for the first time, you will be prompted to indicate whether you want to allow the Mobile App to access your Address Book. If you elect to allow the Mobile App to access your Address Book, the Mobile App will access the names and phone numbers from your Address Book (“Address Book Information”). The Mobile App does not export Address Book Information from your Device.

USAGE INFORMATION

The Mobile App collects the certain usage information that is generated by your use of the Mobile App, such as date, time, numbers called, dial-in passwords, duration for each use of the Mobile App (“Usage Information”). The Mobile App also collects certain information related to the Conferencing Equipment it interacts with and controls, such as equipment and system type, IP address, admin username(s) and admin user password(s) (“Settings Information” and together with Usage Information, “Teleconferencing Information”). The Mobile App uses Teleconferencing Information to connect your Device to and interact with Conferencing Equipment, and to provide you the features of the Mobile App. The Mobile App stores Teleconferencing Information locally to your Device, and the Company does not collect Teleconferencing Information.

DEVICE INFORMATION

Company automatically collects the certain information from your Device. For example, when you use the Mobile App your Device’s operating system, type, Unique User ID (UUID), and wireless carrier for your Device, as well as information about software errors experienced by your Device while using the Mobile App, including the state of your Device at the time of the error (“Device Information”) are automatically logged. Company uses Device Information to improve the Mobile App and to make software updates, product support and other services more relevant to its users.

The Mobile App may also use location services on your device to determine your approximate physical location (“Location Information”). Company may collect and share Location Information and Device Information with our third party service providers to diagnose, analyze and correct software errors, to improve the Mobile App and to make software updates.

OTHER INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

We may also collect the information you provide us from time to time through the following (each of the following, “Volunteered Information”):

  • If/when you contact us via email (with feedback, for support, or otherwise), we will collect your name and email address, as well as any other content included in the email, in order to send you a reply;
  • If/when you participate in one of our surveys, we may collect additional profile information; and
  • We may also collect personal information at other points on our App that states personal information is being collected.
USE OF INFORMATION

In addition to the use of your information as described under the section “Information We Collect”, we may additionally use your information as follows:

  • Generally, we collect, store, use and disclose information to enable us to provide the Mobile App, identify you as a user, respond to your inquiries and emails, improve the Mobile App, send you administrative and service related communications, and send you newsletters, surveys, offers and other promotional materials.
  • Except for the limited disclosures described in this Privacy Policy, we will endeavor to keep your email address private and we do not sell customer lists. We will obtain additional consent prior to any such sale if required under applicable law.
  • We may create anonymous information from your personal information by excluding information (such as your name) that makes the data identifiable to you. We may use anonymous information to analyze request and usage patterns so that we may identify or make improvements to our Mobile App, and/or enhance the content of our Mobile App.
DISCLOSURE AND SHARING OF YOUR INFORMATION

In addition to the disclosure and sharing of your information as described under the section “Information We Collect,” Company may share some or all of the following information with its third party service providers for the purpose of analyzing your use of the Mobile App and improving the Mobile App and Company’s other products and services: Volunteered Information, your Device Information, and general information about your interactions with the Mobile App (collectively, “Information”). For the purposes of clarity, your Information does not include Teleconferencing Information or Address Book Information. Company will only share the minimum portion of your Information necessary to provide the Mobile App and improve the Mobile App relevant to your activity.

Company may also disclose any information you provide or it collects, including your Volunteered Information, as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process, or when Company believes in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect its rights, your safety or the safety of others, or to investigate fraud, or respond to a government request. Company does not have any access to any of the personal information or credit card information that you have provided to Apple in order to register for their services. You should be familiar with the privacy practices of Apple before providing such information.

Finally, you agree that Company may transfer all information you provide, including your Volunteered Information, in the event of a sale, merger, dissolution, bankruptcy, corporate reformation or other corporate restructuring of Company, or in the event that Company sells, assigns, or transfers substantially all of its rights in the Mobile App.
